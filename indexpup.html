WHAT DOES THIS MEAN

You can download and use EasyOS, with no restrictions. How you use it and what you do with it is entirely up to you.

WHAT WOULD YOU USE IT FOR - Once downloaded, load EasyOS onto a USB and boot your computer from the USB. Once running, Easyos will allow you to browse the internet just as any other operating system

The only difference is, you will be given an option to put your chosen browser in a 'contanier'. This means you could use EasyOS to do your online banking with security enabled. No external breaches to extract your information. When finished, EasyOS will give you a option to save your settings or start afresh next time you boot Easyos. No modification to the Host operating system
